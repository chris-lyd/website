Ryan

Co-founder

Ryan is the co-founder of Lightyear Data, leading go-to-market, pricing, and sales. A Northwestern University graduate, he started his career at a top investment bank advising on M&A and restructuring transactions — building financial models, running valuation analyses, and preparing the materials boards and creditor groups use to make high-stakes decisions.

Before founding Lightyear Data, Ryan spent several years living and working independently across South America and Europe, an experience that sharpened the same instinct that drives the company: operate independently, adapt fast, and get to a straight answer without a safety net. At Lightyear Data, he owns the client-facing side of the business, making sure every engagement starts with a clear picture of what's actually happening before a single dashboard gets built.

Email: ryan@lightyeardata.com

Chris

Co-founder & Data Lead

Chris is the co-founder and technical lead of Lightyear Data. A Northwestern University graduate, he led data operations at North America's fastest-growing pet insurance company through five years of scaling and a private equity exit, building the infrastructure that let a fast-growing operator make decisions it could actually trust.

At Lightyear Data, Chris leads the team that designs and builds each client's warehouse, transformation pipelines, and dashboards — guided by the same discipline that defined his earlier work: clean infrastructure, honest numbers, and decisions you can actually trust.
