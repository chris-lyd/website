Questions about pricing

Here's what we hear most often.

Why fixed pricing instead of a custom quote?

Because "it depends" pricing is how consultants pad margin on discovery calls. We've productized the two engagement shapes we actually see with operator businesses. If you don't fit one, we'll tell you before you pay us anything.

What happens after setup?

The monthly subscription covers ongoing pipeline maintenance, dashboard upkeep, automation monitoring, and support — not a one-time build you're left to maintain yourself.

Can I upgrade tiers later?

Yes. A Foundation client upgrading to Operator pays a $5,000 expansion fee, so total implementation fees paid equal the published $10,000 Operator setup price. Monthly service then moves from $1,500 to $2,500. The expansion raises included scope from 4 to 8 sources, 1 to 3 dashboards, and 1 to 4 workflows. Non-standard expansion is scoped as Tailored.

Is there a long-term contract?

No long-term commitment required. Because the warehouse and code are built in your name from day one, there's no technical lock-in — most clients stay because the system keeps paying for itself, not because they're stuck with us.

When do I need the Tailored tier?

If you run multiple entities or brands, need more than 8 data sources, more than 3 dashboards, more than 4 workflows, or have an unusual workflow that does not map to Foundation or Operator. We agree on the roadmap, operating capacity, priorities, and a fixed monthly number before you commit.

Do you offer discounts for annual commitment?

Not currently — we want you to stay because we're delivering value, not because you're locked in. That said, most clients who hit their ROI targets in month 2–3 stay for years.

What counts as a "data source"?

Any system that feeds data into your warehouse — QuickBooks, your CRM, POS, ERP, an API. Foundation includes 4; Operator goes up to 8. Each additional source beyond your tier is $500/mo.

What's an "automated workflow"?

Any repeating task that now runs on a schedule instead of by hand. Examples: daily delinquency alerts, weekly quote submissions, nightly inventory reconciliation. Foundation gets 1 included; Operator gets 4. Each additional is $300/mo.

Do you include support in the subscription?

Yes. Foundation gets email support and monthly check-ins. Operator gets priority support (24–48 hr response) and quarterly business reviews. Both are included in your monthly fee.

Most clients start small and scale up.

Here's the typical journey:

Month 0-1: Foundation

Prove the model on your highest-friction problem — usually a weekly report that takes hours to build. Get one dashboard live, refresh it daily, automate one manual task. Cost: $5K setup + $1.5K/mo.

Month 2-3: Seeing ROI

The first automation is saving 5–8 hours per week. Your team is acting on real numbers instead of rebuilding reports. You're ready to expand — more data sources, more dashboards, more automations.

Month 3+: Upgrade to Operator

Pay a $5K expansion fee, bringing total setup paid to $10K, and move monthly service from $1.5K to $2.5K. Add 4 data sources, up to 2 additional dashboards, and 3 automated workflows.

Ongoing: Scale or Stay

Some clients stay at Operator for years. Some add a third data layer or extend into new verticals — both can happen without changing tiers. Others spin off to neighboring departments (finance, ops, sales). Your stack grows with your business.
