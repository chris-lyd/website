For the mockup, we use publicly available data (like retail numbers or funding info) plus estimates where needed. We don't need access to your systems yet.

That's fine. The mockup is yours to keep either way. We'd rather have you say "not yet" than commit before you're sure it's right.

Usually within 48 hours. We book the call for week 1 or 2, giving us time to build something credible and tailored to your business.
